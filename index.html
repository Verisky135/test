Connect with your pets from anywhere, anytime.

  • ABOUT
  • DETAILS

Sometimes you need to go somewhere for days, but no one can handle your pets at home. They someone to handle the daily need of food and water. IOT PET help you to connect you with your pets even if you're far far away from your home.

Create your own daily schedule or you can manually feed your pet just from your smartphone.

IOT PET control your pet's food dispenser with Intel Galileo microcontroller which connected to your smartphone using internet. You can control it with your smartphone from anywhere anytime as long as you're connected to internet.
